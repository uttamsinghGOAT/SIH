About the System

Our Digital HealthCare platform ensures migrant workers across India have secure, portable, and multilingual access to their medical history, while enabling healthcare providers and policymakers with data-driven insights.

Core Features

Digital Health Passport

Portable QR-based health ID accessible across hospitals nationwide.

AI-Powered Early Warning

Detects disease clusters and alerts health officials in real-time.

Government Scheme Integration

Seamless access to Ayushman Bharat, e-Sanjeevani & other initiatives.

Privacy-First Architecture

Data encryption, role-based access, and GDPR compliance for safety.

Analytics Dashboard

Monitor migrant health trends, vaccinations, and outbreak hotspots.
