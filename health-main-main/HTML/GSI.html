Ayushman Bharat

A flagship health insurance scheme aimed at providing coverage to economically vulnerable families.

  • Health coverage up to ₹5 lakh per family per year
  • Cashless and paperless access to healthcare
  • Wide network of empaneled hospitals and providers

National Health Authority (NHA)

The apex body responsible for implementing India’s public health insurance and digital health ecosystem.

  • Implementation of Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY
  • Health technology assessment and fair pricing
  • Development of the National Digital Health Ecosystem

e-Sanjeevani

A telemedicine service enabling remote doctor consultations, strengthening healthcare access in rural India.

  • Remote video consultation with qualified doctors
  • Digital prescription and medical advice
  • Accessible via web and mobile applications

National Telemedicine Service

A government initiative to expand healthcare reach via telemedicine platforms.

  • Virtual OPD facilities across states
  • Reduced patient travel & cost burden
  • Integration with district-level healthcare centres

National Digital Health Mission (NDHM)

A mission to create an integrated digital health infrastructure for every citizen.

  • Unique Health ID (ABHA) for citizens
  • Unified digital health records
  • Secure sharing of medical history with doctors

Ayushman Bharat Health & Wellness Centres

Centres offering preventive, promotive, rehabilitative and curative care at the community level.

  • Free essential drugs & diagnostic services
  • Focus on maternal & child health
  • Promotion of yoga, nutrition and lifestyle awareness

Health Education and Communication

Government programs to raise awareness and empower citizens with correct health knowledge.

  • Mass awareness campaigns on hygiene & vaccination
  • Multilingual health education materials
  • Community workshops on lifestyle diseases

Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana

A scheme to provide quality medicines at affordable prices through special Janaushadhi stores.

  • Generic medicines at 50–90% lower prices
  • 5000+ operational Janaushadhi Kendras
  • Affordable healthcare for all citizens

Mission Indradhanush

A universal immunization program to cover all children and pregnant women.

  • Free vaccination for 12 vaccine-preventable diseases
  • Special focus on remote & underserved areas
  • Improved maternal & child health outcomes
